FAQ

What is Limerence's style philosophy?
We follow a minimal, detail-driven approach focused on clean silhouettes, neutral tones, and long-lasting quality.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping times and rates vary by location.
How do I know my correct size?
Each product page includes a detailed size guide and fit notes. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for outerwear.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, as long as items remain unworn and in original condition.
